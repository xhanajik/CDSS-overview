RI-01 Patient information
Version 1.0
Author Michaela Hanajíková
Source MUDr. Jana Halámková, Ph.D. (Masaryk Institute of Oncology)
Purpose To accuratly suggest a treatment plan, diagnosis or other predictions and suggestions based on the patients overall health, clinical condition and diagnoses.
Description The system shall store the information corresponding to the patient's personal health information. More precisely:
Specific data -current health condition (sex, age, weight, BMI, ...)
-allergies
-personal anamnesis
-laboratory test results
-imaging reports
-medications and dosing
-abusus
-past surgeries/procedures
-medical history (and family medical history)
-social anamnesis
- . . .
Time interval past and present
Importance crutial
Urgency -
Comments This information should not be manually entered, but automatically taken from the hospitals EHRs, to prevent mistakes and save time.
